Early Life and Education

Mark Edward Fischbach was born on June 28, 1989, on the island of O’ahu, Hawaii. He was born on a military base to an American German father and Korean mother. He also has an older brother, After he was born, the family moved to Cincinnati, Ohio, but his parents divorced when he was young. His father passed away in 2008. Fischbach graduated from Milford High School in 2007 and enrolled at the University of Cincinnati, where he took a biomedical engineering program. He did not complete the program and receive his degree.

In 2012, Mark would undergo a series of personal hardships, including an emergency appendectomy, as well as being hospitalized because of an adrenal tumor. In that same year however, he registered his first YouTube channel on March 6. He originally intended to upload comedy sketches and action videos, but he also had a lifelong interest in video games and decided to do gaming videos while the trend was growing.

Fischbach met Wade Barnes in sixth grade and roomed with Bob Muyskens in his freshman year—they would both go on to become his long-term friends and collaborators.
